Atlantic Warriors

As Atlantic Warriors, we're on a mission to combat plastic pollution.
Powered by cutting-edge technologies like AI and image detection,
we're passionate about making a difference.
Our AI-driven platform detects plastic in images, empowering individuals and organizations to act against this global challenge.
We believe in the collective strength of small actions leading to profound change.
Together, we can preserve the beauty of our oceans, protect wildlife, and create a sustainable future for generations to come.
Join us in the fight for a cleaner, greener world.

Let's turn the tide against plastic pollution, one image at a time.
